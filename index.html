Everything You Need in the Field

Create Spots

Mark prospecting locations with GPS coordinates, custom prefixes, and configurable boundary radius. Use current location or pick from the map.

Sample Tracking

Track soil types, tools used, and material details with large, glove-friendly toggle buttons. Perfect for field conditions.

Auto-Generated IDs

Samples get unique IDs like "WNTB-001" automatically. Just write the short code on your bag - minimal labeling in the field.

GPS Precision

Real-time GPS accuracy indicator shows signal quality. Accept location when accuracy meets your standards for reliable sample positions.

Organized Data

View all spot information at a glance - location, coordinates, radius, and all associated samples. Quick-add new samples with one tap.

Processing Workflow

Track sample progress from 0% to 100% with an intuitive slider. Record finds at any stage and add processing notes.

Record Finds

Log gold, silver, platinum and more with automatic value calculation. Enter weight and see real-time dollar value based on current prices.

Live Metal Prices

Refresh gold, silver, and platinum prices with one tap. Configure currency, units, and customize soil types and tools lists.

Sample Locator

Navigate back to your samples with compass guidance. Real-time distance, bearing, and GPS accuracy help you find samples in the field.
