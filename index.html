Everything You Need in the Field

Spot Management

Mark and organize your prospecting locations with GPS precision. Set boundary radius, add notes, and see distance from your current location.

Sample Tracking

Quick-add samples with auto-generated IDs - minimal labeling in the field. Track soil types, tools used, and material weight with large, glove-friendly buttons.

Processing Workflow

Track progress from collection to fully processed. Update processing percentage in increments with visual status indicators.

Find Recording

Record your discoveries with automatic value calculation. Support for gold, silver, platinum and more with real-time price updates.

Photo Documentation

Capture and tag photos for complete documentation. Smart tags for material, area, bedrock, and more with optimized camera presets.

Field Navigation

Navigate back to samples with audio proximity feedback. Compass direction and real-time distance guide you right to your sample location.
