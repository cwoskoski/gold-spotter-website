Pro Features Explained

Sample Locator

Navigate back to your samples with real-time audio feedback. As you get closer, the beeps speed up. A compass shows the direction, and distance updates in real-time. Essential for relocating samples weeks or months later in areas with many dig sites.

Data Export

Export all your spots, samples, and finds to CSV for spreadsheet analysis. Export GPS waypoints to GPX for use with GPS devices and mapping software. Create backups, analyze patterns, or share locations with prospecting partners.

Custom Lists

Create your own soil types, gathering tools, and photo tags. Add "black sand", "decomposed granite", "sluice box", or any categories that match your prospecting methods. Better organization means better insights.

Multi-Currency

Track find values in USD, CAD, AUD, EUR, or GBP. Auto-fetch current gold, silver, and platinum prices with one tap. See automatic value calculations as you record finds - no manual math needed.
